About Us

We are the Mobile Dudes, we work out of my garage deep in the woods of Kirkland, Washington. It’s beauty is inspirational the fresh air helps us think and the quietness helps us get shit done. It’s not that we don’t enjoy building websites, it’s just that.. well you know, you can go anywhere to get a website these days, where do you go to get a world class app built?… See you can’t think of anyone right?(except us). We made it our mission to build world class mobile apps with a design first thought process, extra emphasis on motion, and visuals . We build our own apps, they get some press, maybe even featured by Apple in the app store, but we can also build apps for other startups, larger companies, and famous people. Why stop at a mobile phone app, need a watch app? or an iPad app? we can do that too, we have 7 years of experience building mobile apps for startups and large tech companies.
